Lingbo Yang graduated from Peking University in 2016 with a Bachelor's Degree in mathematics. Currently he is pursuing a Ph. D. career in Video Coding Laboratory , Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Siwei Ma and Wen Gao. His main research focus lies in cross-media intelligence, especially on human pose/motion transfer, image restoration, and 3D human pose estimation. He has published 3 papers on top-notch multimedia conferences, including ACM Multimedia and two ICMEs (2020 oral).
Besides academic research, he is also keen on sharing the knowledge and recent technological advances with the community with github open-source projects, invited seminar talks and non-profitable subtitling services.
